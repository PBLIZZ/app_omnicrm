1. Purpose of Data Processing

We are requesting your consent to enhance your contact record in our wellness practice management system by collecting your public profile picture from social media platforms. This will help us:

  • Provide a more personalized experience during our sessions
  • Improve our internal contact management and recognition
  • Enhance the visual interface of our practice management system

2. Data We Will Collect

With your consent, we will collect:

  • Social Media Handles/URLs: LinkedIn, Facebook, X (Twitter), Instagram, GitHub profiles associated with your email address
  • Profile Pictures: Publicly available profile pictures from the above platforms
  • Profile URLs: Links to your public social media profiles

Note: We will only access publicly available information. No private or restricted content will be accessed.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

The legal basis for this processing is your explicit consent under Article 6(1)(a) and Article 9(2)(a) of the GDPR.

4. How We Will Use Your Data

  • Display your profile picture in our internal practice management system
  • Link to original profile picture URLs (no local storage of images)
  • Use only for internal practice management and client recognition purposes

We will NOT:

  • Share your profile pictures with third parties
  • Use your images for marketing or promotional purposes
  • Store images locally (only links to original sources)
  • Access private or restricted social media content

5. Data Retention

We will retain links to your profile pictures for as long as you remain an active client. Upon termination of our professional relationship or withdrawal of consent, all profile picture links will be removed from our system within 30 days.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent at any time by contacting us
  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Rectification of inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing of your personal data
  • Data portability - receive your data in a structured format
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

⚠️ Important Information

  • Consent is entirely voluntary - you can decline without affecting your treatment
  • You can withdraw consent at any time
  • This consent is separate from your main treatment consent
  • If you don't consent, we'll use a generic avatar or client-provided photo
